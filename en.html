About us

The Alumni SUISS Association brings together alumni, students, faculty and supporters of the Interdepartmental University School of Strategic Sciences.

The Association promotes cultural and professional relations, enhances the role of Alumni and encourages the exchange of knowledge and expertise at national and international level.

Community

The Alumni SUISS Community is a space for networking, professional exchange and collaboration among members, alumni, students and institutional stakeholders.

Its purpose is to consolidate an active, authoritative network focused on the professional, cultural and institutional growth of its members.

Join the Association

To apply for membership, applicants will be required to complete the membership form and await the assessment of requirements in accordance with the Association Statute.

Alumni, students, faculty members and supporters may join according to the membership categories provided for by the Association Statute.
